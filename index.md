## Quick Start

### For Beginners
1. Start with [Easy machines](machines/easy/) - they teach fundamentals
2. Follow the [Machine Approach Guide](resources/methodology/machine-approach/) for a systematic method
3. Use the [OSCP Prep](resources/cert-prep/) list if you're studying for certs

### For Intermediate Players
1. Focus on [Medium/Hard machines](machines/medium/) by technique
2. Work through a [ProLab](prolabs/) (start with Dante)
3. Attempt [Sherlock](sherlocks/) challenges for blue team skills

### For Advanced Players
1. Target [Insane machines](machines/insane/) and Hard challenges
2. Complete [Cybernetics or APTLabs](prolabs/) ProLabs
3. Write and contribute your own writeups
